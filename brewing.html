French Press

Classic and simple brewing method for rich and full-bodied coffee.

Price: $49

Usage Tips:

  • Use coarse ground coffee to avoid over-extraction.
  • Steep for 4 minutes before pressing the plunger down.
  • Serve immediately after brewing.

Pour-Over

A precise and manual brewing method for balanced coffee flavor.

Price: $37

Usage Tips:

  • Use a medium-fine grind for pour-over coffee.
  • Pour water in a circular motion for even extraction.
  • Allow the coffee to bloom for 30 seconds before continuing to pour.

AeroPress

The AeroPress method consists of steeping coffee for 30-60 seconds and then forcing it through a filter by pushing a plunger through a plastic tube.

Price: $42

Usage Tips:

  • Place a filter paper in the AeroPress and rinse it with hot water to preheat, open up the filter, and remove any residual paper taste.
  • Use a fine-medium grind.
  • Bloom the coffee, then add water and press gently.
